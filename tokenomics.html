Tokenomics
Token Allocation

20% of the supply is reserved for airdrops, marketing initiatives, and development purposes to foster ecosystem growth.80% of the supply is exclusively allocated for distribution to users who contribute to network mapping by mining valid path data.

Token Reward Tiers

1 PTrace Token

Complete path, all hops respond, at least two hops, and a new source/destination ASN pair.

7/8 PTrace Token

Complete path, all hops respond, at least two hops, but not a new source/destination ASN pair.

6/8 PTrace Token

Not a complete path, at least two hops with IP addresses, and a new source and destination ASN pair.

5/8 PTrace Token

Not a complete path, two IPs respond, but not a new ASN pair.

4/8 PTrace Token

Not a complete path, hops with at least two IPs, source and destination ASN are equal.

3/8 PTrace Token

Complete path, at least two hops, not the most observed ASN, and continuous submissions over 24 hours.

2/8 PTrace Token

Complete path, at least two hops, most observed ASN, and continuous submissions over 24 hours.

1/8 PTrace Token

Complete path, at least two hops, and the most observed ASN.

Path Submission Rejection Criteria

Paths will not receive rewards if:The destination IP is private.All paths fail. Latency recorded is zero.
