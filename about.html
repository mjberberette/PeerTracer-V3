About PeerTracer

Welcome to PeerTracer, where we chart the unseen highways and byways of cyberspace. PeerTracer is a collaborative platform that transforms internet exploration into a rewarding experience. By contributing traceroutes, users help map the intricate pathways of the digital world while earning crypto tokens in return.

At PeerTracer, we believe the internet is a vast, interconnected frontier, waiting to be explored and understood. With every submission, you're helping to uncover the routes and connections that power global communication. Together, we're building a dynamic, user-driven map of the online landscape, highlighting its hidden paths and making it more transparent for everyone.
